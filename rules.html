Rule Book

House rules for the Deal or Die League.

Game Play Rules

1. Setup & Dealing

  • All players start with 5 cards.
  • The player to the left of the dealer begins the round.
  • That same player must shuffle the deck at the end of the round.

2. Turn Order & Card Pickup

  • At the start of each turn, 2 cards must be picked up.
  • If a move is made before picking up the 2 cards, the pickup is voided.
  • After picking up cards, the player may play cards or bank cards.
  • Properties cannot be banked.

3. Hand Size Limit & Discards

  • If a player has more than 7 cards at end of turn, other players randomly discard down to 7.
  • Discarded cards are placed into the middle of the deck.

4. Card Commitment & Clarifications

  • Once your hand breaks contact with a card, that action is your official play.
  • For beginners, the group may decide whether to enforce this rule.
  • Rule clarifications are allowed if no strategic advantage is gained.

5. Special Cards & Turn Exceptions

  • “Just Say No” does not consume a turn.
  • “Just Say No” only applies to the player using it.
  • Flipping a wild property to a new color counts as one turn.

6. Houses & Hotels

  • Houses only after a complete set (excluding utilities/railroads).
  • Hotels may be placed on top of houses.
  • Once banked, houses/hotels remain banked.
  • If a house is given as value and there’s no valid place, it goes to bank.

7. Rent & Action Card Rules

  • Double the Rent may be played before or after a rent card.
  • Strongly encouraged: verbalize your move beforehand.

8. Colorless Wildcards

  • No monetary value; can’t be rent payment.
  • If not part of full set, can be taken via Sly Deal / Forced Deal.
  • Must attach to a legitimate property/utility.
  • If attached property is taken due to rent, wildcard stays same color and can’t charge rent unless part of legit property.
  • Can’t form a full set by itself.

9. Card Pickup Errors

  • If extra card is unseen: place on top of deck.
  • If seen: randomly select 1–3 cards, reveal, and shuffle into middle of deck.
